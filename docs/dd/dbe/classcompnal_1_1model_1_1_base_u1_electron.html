Detailed Description

template<typename RealType>
class compnal::model::BaseU1Electron< RealType >

The base class for electron systems with the U(1) symmetry. Conserved quantity is the total sz:
.

\[ \langle\hat{S}^{z}_{\rm tot}\rangle= \sum^{N}_{i=1}\langle\hat{S}^{z}_{i}\rangle \]

and the total electrons

\[ \langle\hat{N}^{\rm tot}_{\rm e}\rangle= \sum^{N}_{i=1}\langle\hat{n}_{i}\rangle\]

.

RealTypeType of real values.
